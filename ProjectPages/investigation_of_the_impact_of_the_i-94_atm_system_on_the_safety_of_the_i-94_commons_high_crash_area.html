Investigation of the Impact of the I-94 ATM System on the Safety of the I-94 Commons High Crash Area

2012-2014 | by John Hourdos, Stephen Zitzow

Purpose:

This report investigates the impact of the Variable Speed Limit (VSL) system on safety in a high-crash area on I-94 westbound through downtown Minneapolis. Using video data, traffic measurements, and machine vision sensors, the study applied a before-and-after analysis to assess crash rates and shockwave activity, aiming to enhance safety by reducing speed variations between upstream and downstream traffic.
